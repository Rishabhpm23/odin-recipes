Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, typically layered with a rich and savory tomato-based meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and generous amounts of melted cheese. The pasta layers are alternated with the sauces and sometimes include additions like ground beef, sausage, or vegetables. The dish is then baked until the pasta is tender and the top layer is golden and bubbly. Lasagna is known for its hearty and comforting flavors, making it a favorite in many households around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 12-15 sheets)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces/425g) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups (about 340g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (about 100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (475ml) béchamel sauce (made with butter, flour, milk, salt, and nutmeg)

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and optional red wine.
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg, season with salt and pepper
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. In a baking dish, layer tomato sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  8. Repeat until ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce and a topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  11. Allow lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing
  12. Serve and enjoy!
